Raigad Fort

Raigad Fort, the stronghold of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, offers both historical significance and spectacular views.

Lohagad Fort

This fort near Lonavala is known for its easy-to-climb hill and panoramic views of the Sahyadri mountain range.

Sindhudurg Fort

A historic naval fort built by Shivaji Maharaj, Sindhudurg stands on an island off the coast of Malvan in the Arabian Sea.

Torna Fort

Torna Fort is one of the oldest forts in Maharashtra, offering a challenging trek and mesmerizing mountain views.
